Privacy Policy

Last Updated: July 2026

Thank you for using wealthOS. Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how wealthOS handles your information.

Information We Collect

The current version of wealthOS does not collect, transmit, sell, or share your personal information.

Financial Data

All financial information you enter into wealthOS—including assets, investments, values, notes, and related data—is stored locally on your device using SQLite.

Your financial information is never transmitted to our servers because wealthOS currently does not use cloud storage or online synchronization.

Internet Connection

wealthOS can function completely offline. An internet connection is not required for normal operation.

Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal or financial information with third parties.

Advertisements

The current version of wealthOS does not display advertisements.

Analytics

wealthOS does not use analytics services or tracking technologies in its current version.

Security

Although we implement reasonable security practices, users are responsible for maintaining the security of their own devices.

Children's Privacy

wealthOS is not intended for children under the age of 13.

Future Features

If future versions introduce cloud synchronization, user accounts, analytics, or additional online services, this Privacy Policy will be updated accordingly.

Changes to this Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be published on this page.
